# Tally Logo Design

**Date:** 2026-03-25

## Goal

Create a warm, minimal SVG logo for Tally that feels local, analytical, and calm rather than sharp or highly technical.

## Approved Direction

Use a data-mark approach instead of a lettermark.

- No text in the mark
- Warm/minimal tone
- Soft calendar-tile structure
- Rounded geometry
- Tally's existing cream and terracotta palette

## Visual Structure

- Rounded outer tile
- Subtle internal 3x3 grid
- A small set of highlighted cells to imply tracked activity over time
- Very light borders and quiet contrast

## Palette

- Cream background: `#FFFFEB`
- White inner panel: `#FFFFFF`
- Border: `#E8E8E0`
- Primary accent: `#CC785C`
- Optional secondary accent: `#7B8CEA` used sparingly or omitted

## Constraints

- Must work as a standalone SVG asset
- Must remain readable at small sizes
- Must avoid heavy detail, gradients, or decorative text
